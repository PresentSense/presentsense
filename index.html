Project Overview

PresentSense is an AI tool designed to aid academics, students, and other public presenters in improving and honing their presentation techniques. The assistant uses state-of-the-art technologies like computer vision and machine learning.

The project allows users to select their preferred presentation style, taking into account their time limits and preferences, before beginning presentation analysis. Subsequently, to provide post-presentation feedback, the speech is aligned with the presentation document that the user uploads.

While analyzing the speaker, the system evaluates the speaker's tone, speech patterns, and facial expressions. As a result, it assists users in enhancing their delivery, which includes modifying their tone, gestures, and slide content.

By analyzing the feedback that presenters receive after submitting their presentations to the system, PresentSense provides customized recommendations to enhance the overall impact of their presentations.
